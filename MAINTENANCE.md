# Maintenance commitment

Prime Beat intends to maintain the public fixture format and reference verifier for at least twelve
months after the first `v0.1.0` release.

- Review AP2 and x402 upstream changes at least monthly.
- Publish compatibility notes before changing a pinned source version.
- Keep prior fixture versions available and avoid silent expected-result changes.
- Triage security reports within five business days.
- Mark unsupported versions explicitly instead of presenting stale fixtures as current.
